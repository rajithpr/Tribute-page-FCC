Mohanlal

The complete actor of the Indian film industry.

Here's a time line of Mohanlal's life.

  • 1960 - Born in Pathanamthitta, Kerala, India.
  • 1978 - Debut movie titled Thiranottam released after 25 years.
  • 1980 - was cast in the movie Manjil Virinja Pookkal as villain. Film was a great success.
  • 1986 - The gangster hero role in the movie Rajavinte Makan established him as the superstar.
  • 1991 - National and State award for Best Actor for the movie Bharatham.
  • 1995 - State award for Best Actor.
  • 1999 - National and State award for Best Actor for the movie Vanaprastham,
  • 2001 - Honoured with Padma Shri by Government of India.
  • 2005 - State award for Best Actor for the movie Thanmathra.
  • 2007 - State award for Best Actor for the movie Pardesi.
  • 2009 - Honorary Lieutenant Colonel by Indian Army.
  • 2010 - Honorary Doctor of Letters (D.Litt.) by Sree Sankaracharya University of Sanskrit.
  • 2018 - Honorary Doctor of Letters (D.Litt.) by University of Calicut.
  • 2019 - Honoured with Padma Bhushan by Government of India.
